# Chapter Intent

## Goal
This 40-chapter novel is structured as **4 arcs of 10 chapters each**, functioning like four compact volumes inside one book. This fits tomato pacing: fast hook, steady gains, clear cliffhangers, and escalating reveals.

## Outline Node
This 40-chapter novel is structured as **4 arcs of 10 chapters each**, functioning like four compact volumes inside one book. This fits tomato pacing: fast hook, steady gains, clear cliffhangers, and escalating reveals.

## Must Keep
- Cities survive on dungeon ore, salvage, relic-tech, alchemical fungus, monster materials, and purified cores.
- Guilds control entry points, licensing, maps, and legal salvage rights.

## Must Avoid
- No hidden royal bloodline or destiny reveal that invalidates earned progression
- No sudden overpowering relic that solves combat without tradeoff
- No repetitive full-page stat sheets every chapter
- No faction existing solely to praise or follow Rowan
- No resurrection fake-out that erases sacrifice
